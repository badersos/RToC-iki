Azure Tiger Saint

Sect Master Of The Azure Heaven Creation Sect

“People, from birth to growth, receive immeasurable grace that cannot be expressed in words. The reason I have upheld righteousness and maintained my version of goodness is because it is the duty and responsibility of everyone who has received grace throughout their life. No, it is not a duty but a privilege... To die for the noble ideal that righteousness defines. Gazing upon righteousness, knowing how to mend our wrongs. To love each other with purity and goodwill. To have pride in impossible dreams. Believing thus, reaching the stars.”
— Cheongmun Sunwoo's Final Words to Oh Hyeon-seok
Cheongmun Sunwoo (창호자), known as the Azure Tiger Saint, was the founder of the Azure Heavenly Sect. Renowned for his unwavering righteousness and immense fighting prowess, he served as a mentor to Seo Eun-hyun in the 14th Cycle.

Overview

Cheongmun Sunwoo founded the Azure Heavenly Sect and led it with a philosophy of righteousness and grace. He is called the Azure Tiger Saint due to his formidable martial arts and spiritual power.

In the 14th Cycle, Eun-hyun joined the Azure Heavenly Sect and trained under Cheongmun Sunwoo. Sunwoo sacrificed himself by self-destructing to defend the Bright Cold Realm from a combined attack by The Esteemed One from the Blood Yin Realm and The Black Dragon.

He was initially thought to have died due to Heavenly Tribulation during his advancement to Integration. However, it was later revealed that a [White Deer] took him, saving him from his untimely death. His current condition remains unknown.
