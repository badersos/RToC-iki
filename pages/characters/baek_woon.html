Baek Woon is the Sacred Master of the Bright Cold Realm. She is a fervent devotee of [Light]. She is also known to call upon the powers of the Eight Radiance's Immortals, notably that of the Fifth Seat, and the Seventh Seat.

Overview

Baek Woon commands respect as the Sacred Master of the Bright Cold Realm. Her devotion to Light influences her every action and decision.
