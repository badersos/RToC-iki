Black Dragon

The True Yin True Lord

"He lacked talent and could not turn completely black. He strived, strived, and strived again and became an Imoogi, then a True Dragon." — Narrator
The Immortal Beast Black Dragon (黑龍), formerly known as a simple Snake, is the Immortal Lord of True Yin. His true form is a dragon capable of encircling a galaxy made of darkness.
He was born as a snake and cultivated with all of his being to become a member of the Demon Race and gained recognition among the Snake Race. He shed his skin to become a Black Snake that is revered by the Snake Race.

Overview

He lacked talent and could not turn completely black. He strived, strived, and strived again and became an Imoogi, then a True Dragon. He is the Immortal Lord of True Yin.

Appearance

Their normal appearance is that of a Chinese Dragon, sometimes of dark blue hue, but often cloaked in darkness no matter what form they assume. A massive dragon of darkness, seemingly capable of coiling around an entire galaxy.

Relationships

Allies: Oh Hye-seo (Former)

Enemies: Seo Eun-hyun
