Blood Yin

Nether Devil True Lord's Leftover

"I have aided the workings of the Imperial Venerable...with my own hands. With that...I have no regrets..." — Blood Yin
Blood Yin was the leftover of the Nether Devil True Lord, Yu Hao Te, after the latter perished. They aimed to return to the position Yu Hao Te held in the Underworld, through the Seat of Vice they held.
Blood Yin self-destructed after a battle with Seo Eun-hyun, pushing the latter into his True Immortal advancement, and themselves gaining rest.

History

The Blood Yin and True Devil Realm

In the distant past, when the True Devil Realm was whole, the Devil Realm was divided into two factions: the Demonic Devils and the Heavenly Fiends. Among them, the Heavenly Fiends would consume Demonic Devils as resources through their peculiar devilish arts. They were especially known for techniques that eroded the minds of Demonic Devils, corrupting and turning them into eternal slaves of the Heavenly Fiends.

The Yuchok Tribe was once enslaved by the Heavenly Fiends, but one of their ancestors countered the mind erosion by parasitizing the Heavenly Fiends' mind in reverse with clear self-awareness, battling their mental power with a technique that became the origin of Mysterious Bizarre Gu.

Esteemed Ones
