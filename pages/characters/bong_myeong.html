Bong Myeong

The Liberation Supreme Deity

Bong Myeong (Gongmyo Cheon-saek), the Liberation Supreme Deity, is the youngest Supreme Deity, having ascended only forty thousand years ago. Ruler of the Earth Boundary Heavenly Domain, their symbol is the [Bird Trapped in a Cage].

Overview

Bong Myeong governs over the realms of Qi Refining 6th Star and Sacred Vessel. Originally a full Supreme Deity, they were demoted to a half-step Supreme Deity after the timelines were split by the Radiance Mantra.

They are also known as the Owner of Liberation. Despite their high status, they are the youngest among the Supreme Deities and have a unique connection to the concept of liberation and circuitry.

Techniques & Abilities

Liberation Peach Garden Village

Bong Myeong's signature Immortal Art. It consists of a world inside a painting where countless beings are trapped. These beings are promised release if they fulfill their assigned roles for 100 years. By acting diligently, they accumulate experience points and increase their rank, gaining special privileges.

However, the world operates under unique rules that gradually consume those trapped within. The karma accumulated from playing their roles solidifies, effectively trapping them further. Within this domain, standard attraction force, immortal arts, cultivation, and manifestation cannot be used. Those who lose all power become wandering spirits.

Imperial Throne Circuitry

A unique cultivation method created by Bong Myeong. It involves creating circuits using star veins across an entire Heavenly Domain. These circuits amplify the power drawn from the Origin Essence of Liberation. It is described as being similar to the Mad Lord's circuits, but evolved a quadrillion times in complexity and power.
