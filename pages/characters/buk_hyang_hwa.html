Buk Hyang-hwa

Conqueror King

"Was what I just gave you also a curse?"
Buk Hyang-hwa was a magical artifacts artisan and a holder of the Law Pattern Talent. She was Seo Eun-hyun's wife in the 10th Cycle and designed his Colourless Glass Swords.

Overview

Due to Eun-hyun’s actions in the 12th cycle, she became a prominent figure in the Head Realm, later ascending to Ancient Force Realm alongside Kim Young-hoon, Seo Ran and Song Jin.

As the Conqueror King, she was an ally to the Wuji Religious Order. She was among the twenty-three of Eun-hyun’s comrades to survive the Owner of Great Mountain.

Due to the backlash she faced from her Law Pattern Talent upon investigating the connection between her norigae and Eun-hyun’s Canvas of Myriad Forms and Connections, she died, with her soul turning into salt and entering the Dream World left by the Owner of Salt Mountain.

Appearance

Buk Hyang-hwa is described as a serene looking woman with jet black hair. She wore white robes, a jewelled bracelet and a jade norigae on her waist.

Due to the effect of the curse of the Law Pattern Talent, she begins to look like an old woman.

History with Seo Eun-hyun

Buk Hyang-hwa had her first encounter with Seo Eun-hyun in the Heaven Treading Desert, where she gave him water to sate his thirst. In the next cycle, she finds Kim Young-hoon and Seo Eun-hyun after they recently arrived at Cheon-Saek city and helps them get water and new clothing. Seo Eun-hyun later enlisted her help to create a formation he needed.
