Golden Shaking Bird

Attendant Concubine of Yang Su-jin

"Well. If that's the case, it can't be helped, can it? I will follow you. I will chase you to the Head Realm where you have gone, chase you to your homeland, and kill every person precious to you. Until the only person you have left to look at is me!" — Golden Shaking Bird (金振鳥)
The Immortal Beast Golden Shaking Bird (金振鳥) is the attendant concubine and pet of Yang Su-jin. She is located in the Lightning Sacred Sea, the Decomposing Corpse Realm of Yang Su-jin's left ring finger, plugging the 'starting point' of Sun and Moon Heavenly Domain.

Overview

She had been instructed by her master that, should someone inherit the Extinguishing Divine Tribulating Heavens Technique of the Golden Divine Heavenly Thunder Sect and visit the Lightning Sacred Sea, the management space of the realm should be gifted to them. Seo Eun-hyun, having learned this technique in the 17th cycle, qualified her criteria.

Appearance

Golden Shaking Bird's true body is a hummingbird whose body is composed entirely of lightning.

Her human transformation is a small girl with platinum blonde hair, dressed in a robe made of golden lightning. She has pristinely white and clean bare feet.
