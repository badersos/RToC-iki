Gwak Am (गा ॐ)

Great Mountain Supreme Deity

": : Bow to me. Lower your head. The one who inherits Master's will...across Heaven, Earth, and Heaven's above, is I alone. : :"
— Gwak Am
The Red Summit Great Mountain Life Phenomena Extinguishing Great Emperor गा ॐ (Gwak Am) is the Great Mountain Supreme Deity. They are the Supreme Deity of the Dao of Repentant Enlightenment, and their symbol is the [Mountain (山) Exuding Darkness].

Overview

They do not have a Heavenly Domain under Their direct command, but have claimed the Earth-Axis Heavenly Domain and stuffed it with their Corpse Mountain Blood Seas.

They govern the realms of Qi Refining 3rd Star, Qi Refining 14th Star, Four Axis/Earth Axis, and Upper Immortal.

They were the former Field Garden Heavenly Lord of the Radiance Heavenly Lords, until Their defection. They are the only disciple that the Salt Sea Supreme Deity accepted in Their lifetime. They are the administrator of the Penglai Dream World, and the former master of the Ender known as Oh Hye-seo.

History

In their mortal days, they were a mixed-blood child of a commoner Fighting Ghost Race father with one horn and a Human Race mother. Their courtesan mother was violated by their father near a temple. They were born as a result of this twisted union.

The name 'गाॐ' was given to them by their mother when they were born and means to "sing to heaven", and because of this, he could never escape from fate.

Personality

He has a very light view of life, as seen by his Corpse Mountain Blood Sea and lack of hesitation to destroy the entirety of Mount Sumeru, and has a deep hatred for anyone other than his master.

According to Seo Eun-hyun, the Gwak Am who isn't in an explosive mood is the most dangerous.

Relationships

Salt Sea Supreme Deity

They had met them after their mother's death in a state of confusion before being raised as the next Field Garden Heavenly Lord. They are the being he respects most in the world.

Oh Hye-seo

They viewed her as nothing but a pawn to be manipulated. They used her as a sword for themself by using Oh Hye-seo's hatred towards Seo Eun-hyun and giving false promises to resurrect Seo Hweol in order to manipulate her.

Seo Eun-hyun

Their relationship with him is antagonistic. They don't recognise Seo Eun-hyun as their master's disciple and see him as a bug and an insult to their master's name.

Appearance

Gwak Am in the Dream World wrapped themself in bandages and dressed in rags.

While paying their respects to their master, They had a head full of long, purple hair, and wore a robe embroidered with screaming faces.

During his battle with Seo Eun-Hyun he wore blood-red robes and a golden mask. Beneath the golden mask is his face, with 17 scars, the place where his 17 horns of the fighting ghost race previously were.
