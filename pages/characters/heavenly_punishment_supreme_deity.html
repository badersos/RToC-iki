Heavenly Punishment Supreme Deity

The Nine Heavens Origin Responding Thunderous Sound Universal Transformation Great Emperor

“The time to wash away the shame has arrived. Cry out, O Essence of Great Heavenly Punishment.”
— Heavenly Punishment Supreme Deity
Nine Heavens Origin Responding Thunderous Sound Universal Transformation Great Emperor Do Gon (구천응원뇌성보화천존), simply known as the Heavenly Punishment Supreme Deity, is the God of all lightning and the Namer of Indra's Net. They are the former ruler of the Bearing Tree Heavenly Domain and govern over most advancement rituals in the system of Immortal Cultivation.

Overview

Do Gon is the Heavenly Punishment Supreme Deity. Their symbol is the [Lightning Spear]. They govern over most advancement rituals in the system of Immortal Cultivation, from Qi Refining towards Entering Nirvana, and adjusted the heavenly tribulations accordingly. Prior to their existence, heavenly tribulations were much more disastrous.

During a massive fight against Radiance Hall, their entire faction was killed, and They Themselves were imprisoned in the Horse Ear Heavenly Domain. They managed to escape after retrieving the light bulb from the 1003rd cycle.

Personality

Do Gon is a complex deity who cares deeply for their "daughter" Zhengli. Though powerful and commanding as the God of Lightning, they have suffered great loss, including their entire faction.

Relationships

Zhengli

She is born from the remnants of the past Red Pearl Heavenly Kings that Do Gon gathered to make a banner which they then breathe their split soul into it, becoming Immortal Treasure of the Heavenly Punishment Supreme Deity Do Gon.

However, she was a personality born from the split soul due to unknown reasons and became an entirely new existence which they then named "Zhengli" and cherished it like a daughter.
