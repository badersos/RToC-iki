Heavenly Venerable of the Underworld

The Oldest One

The Netherworld Queen Mother Bong Hwa is the Western Heavenly Venerable, and her symbol is the [White Wheel]. She is also known as the Heavenly Venerable of the Underworld, the [Oldest One], the Death God, and the Master of the Netherworld.

Overview

She is the Heavenly Venerable in charge of the Underworld.

She governs over the Entering Nirvana realm and the True Immortal Advancement.

She helps Enders that meet her standards and taxidermies those who don't. She holds Authority like Sal Tree Heavenly Venerable and Heavenly Venerable of Time. In addition, She holds 14 Origin essences on top of the one that She has control of.

The ultimate goal of her and the Salt Sea Supreme Deity is to trace back the History of the World using the Wheel of Reincarnation and reach the time before the [First Seat] of light in order to both transcend it and ascend to True Emperor.
She had attempted to advance to the Life Supreme Deity, however, despite being called the [Oldest One], she has less history than the Future King, meaning she is unqualified. To circumvent this, She, along with Salt Sea, created the Wheel and Reincarnation to trace her previous lives using History Induction, then piggybacked off of that to read her past lives when the Third Absolute governed reincarnation. By adding these histories onto her own, Her history would eventually surpass the Future King’s, giving Her the qualification, which is why she has to preserve her power. This also fulfils the achievement, as the History Revision brought in a reincarnation system, a feat greater than the Absolute of History.
