Heo Gwak

Elder of Black Ghost Valley

Heo Gwak (허곽), known by the alias White Bone Ghost Devil, was an Elder of the Head Realm faction of the Black Ghost Valley. A peer to formidable figures like Cheongmun Sunwoo and Jin Byuk-ho, Heo Gwak played a pivotal role in the mentor-ship of Kang Min-hee.

Overview

Heo Gwak was a prominent Elder of the Black Ghost Valley in the Head Realm. They discovered Kang Min-hee's rare [Ghostly Yin Transformation] spiritual root and took her under their wing, nurturing her potential.

In the 20th and 21st regressions, Heo Gwak displayed immense selflessness. Along with all the Elders of the Black Ghost Valley from the Head Realm, they sacrificed themselves and the Nether Crossing Ship to seal the Holy Ghost Guiding Mother, a calamitous entity threatening the realm.
