Heuk Sa

The Radiance Supreme Deity

"That is not something a thing like you needs to know." — Hong Fan, to Seo Eun-hyun
Heuk Sa is a title carried by the Radiance Supreme Deity. However, as the Radiance Supreme Deity is actually comprised of the ten Heavenly Lords who jointly occupy the [Seat of Radiance], the Radiance Ten Heavenly Lords, it actually refers to them as a group.
They rule over the Heavenly King Heavenly Domain and seek for the completion of a story.

Overview

They are known as the Owner of Light and the God of Light. They govern the realms of Qi Refining 4th Star, Vestige Liberation Immortal, and Immortal Lord. They are the ones who grant and take away the Fates of the Enders.

The Radiance Seven Immortals

After the defection of the Castle Wall Heavenly Lord, Field Garden Heavenly Lord, and Sword Spear Heavenly Lord (the former tenth, ninth, and fifth seats), the Radiance Ten have been reduced to the Radiance Seven Immortals:
