Hyeon Go

The Naming Supreme Deity

"In the absence of this Immortal, it was far more difficult for everyone to walk their [Path] through a fitting Immortal Title. The name of this Immortal is an unparalleled blessing to you. Receive a name from this Immortal, and walk the path of an Immortal more firmly."
Hyeon Go, also known as the Naming Supreme Deity, was once a human who, in the age before Heaven Tribe cultivation methods existed, reached the pinnacle of the Earth Tribe and became the Immortal Beast King. They are the God of Names and the Master of all Contracts and Oaths.
They rule over the Elephant Nose Heavenly Domain and govern the point immediately after the Immortal Ascension and the cultivation formulas for the Heaven Tribe.

Overview

All of the Heaven Tribe are essentially extensions of the Naming Supreme Deity. Until they become Vestige Liberation Immortals and transcend their birth nature, the authority to oversee celestial energy for mortal beings belongs solely to the Naming Supreme Deity.

They considered Seo Eun-hyun their sibling.

True Immortals

Hyeon Go is critically important for the cultivation and advancement of True Immortals, granting them a more stable and certain path to travel when advancing. All True Immortals gain a [name] from them after they advance, hastening their cultivation. The cultivation formulas for each realm are created by the Naming Supreme Deity.

Black Ancient Paper

A mysterious artifact connected to Hyeon Go's power over names and contracts.

Dreams, the power of the Ancient (古; Go)

The power associated with the "Ancient" (Go) aspect of Hyeon Go's existence, connected to dreams and the primordial nature of creation.

Trivia

  • If a human becomes an Immortal Beast, the name Go (古/Ancient) is attached to them.
  • Hyeon Go's title is inspired by Zhao Xuanlang, a Daoist God from the Song Dynasty, whose posthumous title is "High Spirit Grand Dao Nine Heavens Overseeing Fate Preserving Life Heavenly Venerable Great Emperor". Tremendous swapped Heavenly Venerable to Celestial.
