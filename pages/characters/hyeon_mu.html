Hyeon Mu

The True Martial Great Emperor

"At last, you've fully stepped upon it. Upon Udumbara (優曇婆羅). Welcome to hell." — Hyeon Mu, Heavenly Venerable of the Void
Hyeon Mu (玄武), also known as the True Martial Great Emperor or the Northern Heavenly Venerable, is the Heavenly Venerable in charge of the Interdimensional Void. She is a Martial Artist who has reached the Seventh Manifestation.

Overview

Her symbol is the [Blackened Three Great Ultimate's]. She governs over the Great Net realm, as well as the Manifestation and Martial Realms starting from the Pinnacle Realm.

Spirits are originally born from the third Absolute, live their lives in the world, and upon death, return to the third Absolute. If the third Absolute is absent, they melt into the domain of purity. Without the reincarnation system, all beings become [one] within the third Absolute.

That is the ideal world the Heavenly Venerable of Void argued for. For if death means becoming one with the third Absolute, then that itself can be called the ultimate of Martial Arts... All beings could reach Martial Pinnacle just by dying.
