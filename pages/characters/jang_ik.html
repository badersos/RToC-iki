Jang Ik

Heaven-Collapsing Esteemed One

"Be honored to know, child. I am the supreme leader of the Heart Tribe, Heaven-Collapsing Esteemed One, Jang Ik (Radiant Wing)."
Jang Ik is known as the Heaven-Collapsing Esteemed One — the only Second Step Before the Throne cultivator in the Heart Tribe and their supreme leader. He is at least 40,000 years old.

Description

He is described as green-skinned, ears as large as fans, nose as big as a fist, with jagged teeth and sharp fingernails, and incredibly muscular under tattered clothes. His weapon of choice are four podaos called the Four Treasures Annihilating Heavens Blade.

In the same way Seo Eun-hyun and Kim Young-hoon called it Martial Arts, Jang Ik's Heart Path is called Fighting Spirit. He has 23 direct disciples (with Tae Yeol-jeon being the 23rd disciple during the 18th cycle and Seo Eun-hyun being the 23rd disciple during the 19th cycle).

Techniques

Fighting Spirit: The term Jang Ik uses for Heart Path Method.

Four Treasures Annihilating Heavens Blade (四寶滅天刀):

  • Executing Immortals Annihilating Heavens (誅仙滅天)
  • Slaughtering Immortals Annihilating Heavens (戮仙滅天)
