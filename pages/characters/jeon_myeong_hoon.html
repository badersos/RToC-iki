Jeon Myeong-hoon is one the Enders and the second of this generation to reach the level of Heavenly King. As soon as he transmigrated, he was taken by the Golden Divine Heavenly Thunder Sect due to his Heavenly Golden Thunder Body. He is also known as the Heaven-Destroying One.

Overview

Jeon Myeong-hoon was the nephew of the Executive Director of the company where Seo Eun-hyun worked. His lavish upbringing and nepotism at the company made him lax and lazy, often handing over his tasks to Eun-hyun while he read web novels.

On Earth, he was arrogant and harassed Seo Eun-hyun. However, in the Middle Realms, after the Golden Divine Heavenly Thunder Sect was destroyed and his lover Jin So-hae died, he went mad and became a mass murderer known as the Slaughterer Demon. In the 19th Cycle, he became the Chief Law Protector of the Wuji Religious Order.

Appearance

His Gandhara is like the Vajra That Removes Filth.

Personality

Jeon Myeong-hoon was arrogant and selfish, always shirking responsibility. He manipulated others to carry his burdens while he sought attention. He lacked conscience or shame, blaming others for his failures. Quick to anger and insult, he thrived on authority without accountability. In crisis, he was loud, aggressive, yet morally hollow.
