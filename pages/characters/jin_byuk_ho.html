Jin Byuk-ho

Supreme Leader of Golden Heavenly Divine Sect

Jin Byuk-ho is the Supreme Leader of the Golden Heavenly Divine Sect and a powerful cultivator reaching the Four-axis stage.

Overview

Jin Byuk-ho made his debut in Chapter 3 of the Manhwa. As the leader of the Golden Heavenly Divine Sect, he holds immense authority and power within the cultivation world.
