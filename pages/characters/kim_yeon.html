Kim Yeon

Tridacna Vast Cold Heavenly King

"Why? Why does he get to die happily, while we have to live in the life he ruined? It's too unfair... We can't let this be... He should also experience the agony of his lifelong goal crumbling before his eyes..."
Kim Yeon is an Ender. She is the only disciple of the Mad Lord, Jo Yeon. She is the only holder of the Liberation Law Talent, student of the Liberation Supreme Deity Bong Myeong and a Great Net Immortal.
Kim Yeon is one of Seo Eun-hyun's colleagues that transmigrated with him. She fell in love with Seo Eun-hyun even before transmigrating and is one of the love interests of Eun-hyun. She is kind and cares deeply for her colleagues as even after she went mad, she kept tabs on them.
She was taken by the Mad Lord after awakening a Nascent Soul consciousness in the Head Realm. Kim Yeon's biggest achievement is her ability to unleash the final phase of the Wonderfully Mysterious Fortress, Yeon's Play. After attaining Five Energies Converging at the Origin in the 19th cycle, Kim Yeon sees her own colors of the seven emotions.
In the 13th Cycle, she lived with Seo Eun-hyun for a thousand years after he became a puppet. In the end, she activated Yeon's Play. In the 19th Cycle, she was rescued by Seo Eun-hyun and his group; After which, they fell into the Nether Ghost Realm. The group established the Wuji Religious Order, where she became the Left Law Protector and was named the Mysterious Bizarre Ghost King.

Appearance

Kim Yeon is only described as 'a cute junior'. She is implied to possess a large breast size.

Post-transmigration, she typically dresses in pink robes.

As a True Immortal of the Great Net rank, she is described as a giant god formed of pale pink light.

Her Gandhara is like the Mahasthamaprapta Bodhisattva.

Personality

Kim Yeon is kind and cares deeply for her colleagues. Even after she went mad, she kept tabs on them, showing her underlying compassion despite her descent into madness. Her love for Seo Eun-hyun is unwavering, having spent 1,000+ years with him in the 13th Cycle which only strengthened her feelings.

Detailed Relations

Seo Eun-hyun: Kim Yeon fell in love with Seo Eun-hyun even before transmigrating. In the 13th Cycle, she lived with him for a thousand years after he became a puppet, strengthening her love for him immensely. Though this is of course lost to regression. According to the author, Eun-hyun was supposed to reject Kim Yeon's feelings but due to the overwhelming Korean readers' support, he made Eun-hyun reciprocate with her. They are now married.
