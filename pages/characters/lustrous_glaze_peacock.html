Lustrous Glaze Peacock

The Ecstasy True Lord

"They walk the Dao of Addiction." — Description
The Lustrous Glaze Peacock (琉璃孔雀) is an Immortal Beast who walks the Dao of Addiction. They are notorious for capturing and violating entities with no care for sex, species, or sexual preferences.
They previously managed to steal the seat from the Azure Peng by violating them for countless years. Azure Peng eventually retook it with the help of others.

Overview

Glass Peacock's interpretation of the Dao of Addiction is so strong, they are comparable to an Immortal Lord, despite no longer holding their seat. Later on, during copulation with Azure Peng, the two half-mixed and became the Ecstasy True Lord (歡樂眞君).

Relationships

Relatives: Azure Peng (Previous Reincarnation, Sisters), Yeo Hwi (Descendant)

Students: Seo Eun-hyun (1000th Cycle)

Enemies: Azure Peng (Former)
