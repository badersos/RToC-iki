Obsidian Devil Heavenly King

The God of Gods

Obsidian, or the Obsidian Devil Heavenly King, was one of the three most powerful Enders and was referred to as The God of Gods. They were the original owner of the Flawless Mantra, which eventually became the Swallowing Heaven Supreme Deity. Their lingering thought became the Immortal Beast Supreme Tiger (太虎). His true name is Je Pa.

Overview

Je Pa comes from a world where demigods exist. He himself was a demigod with a level of power equivalent to the Star Shattering Realm.

Appearance

A tiger-person with skin and fur so dark that it’s difficult to discern their form.

Trivia

  • Je Pa’s guide was his wife.
  • The creation of the Swallowing Heaven Supreme Deity through the Impeccable Mantra after his death was not intentional.
