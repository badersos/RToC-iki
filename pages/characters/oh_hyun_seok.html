Oh Hyun-seok

Silver Nameless Heavenly King

"Someday, I will definitely reunite with my entire family, and together...we'll laugh."
Oh Hyun-seok is one of Seo Eun-hyun's colleagues that transmigrated with him. He was one of the only people Seo Eun-hyun relied on the most back when they were on Earth. He was taken by the Azure Heaven Creation Sect when the sect leader Cheongmun Sunwoo found that he possessed a legendary physique called the Unique Holy Body.

Overview

Oh Hyun-seok possessed a legendary physique called the Unique Holy Body. This physique allows him to absorb any type of energy and incorporate it into the primordial chaos he is cultivating, thus elevating his realm. His training in the infamously harsh Azure Heaven Creation Sect led him to enjoy training that is no different from torture.

He worked incredibly hard in the 14th Cycle to reach Eun-hyun's level. The Unique body let him get stronger quickly. In the 13th Cycle, he completely threw away everything and engrossed in women and alcohol after his master died.

Appearance

His Gandhara is like the Benevolent Kings.

When absorbing Heaven and Earth ghostly energy, his entire body turns a shade of purple due to his Unique Holy Body.
