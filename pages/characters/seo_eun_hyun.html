Seo Eun-hyun

The Crystal King Ender Supreme Deity

"I thought I had buried the memories of my past life, but how could human emotions be simply buried?"
Seo Eun-hyun is an Ender of the Supreme Deity rank, as well as the Immortal Beast King, called Star Genesis Supreme Deity walking on the path of the Dao of Myriad Stars. He is the Owner of Myriad Stars with the symbol of [Galaxy with Three Tails]. He is the first Ender ever to independently form a soul and step into the rank of Supreme Deity.
He governs over the Qi Refining stages of 1st Star, 2nd Star and 7th Star, the Realms of Qi Building, Core Formation, Earth Axis, Star Shattering, Sacred Vessel, Vestige Liberation Immortal, Upper Immortal and Great Net Immortal. He also governs the divine power of the Entering Nirvana Realm.
In the 2010th Cycle, he succeeded in scattering himself through the Domain of Purity, creating and becoming his own unique cultivation system, Paper Flower.
During the battle against the Future King Fate Supreme Deity, he seized control of the Absolute of Miracle, Sincerity by absorbing the Absolute Fragments of Miracle that existed within every Ender. The missing Desire Fragment was replaced with a fake Absolute Fragment of Crystal Glass created through Oh Hye-seo’s Truth Manipulation, though the Future King willingly handed over the Desire Fragment not long after. Through this act, he ascended to the rank of True Emperor, becoming the Crystal King Ender Supreme Deity and the Owner of Miracles.

Appearance

His True Body is a being made of Glass True Fire with horns, a dragon tail and sharp, jagged claws on his hands and feet. He wears a white robe woven out of Glass Swords and carries a Wheel made of starlight with a Pure White Three Great Ultimates inside it on his back. After consuming the Immortal beast Pangu True Blood left by Hyeon Rang, his upper body transformed into that of a human.

As the Immortal Beast King, He appears to be wrapped around Mount Sumeru in a double helix, with His head resting at the summit, gazing down at the mountain from above.

His appearance as the Star Genesis Supreme Deity has skin so pale it is almost transparent, with seas of stars and galaxy clusters flowing beneath it; his whole body is seemingly composed of stars.

Personality

His personality is at the level of being considered a person of character. Even though the pain that accumulates with each repeated return is expressed in the image of a transparent hell, he considers life and all the moments within it to be a blessing and believes that it should not be wasted. 'At the same time, I even believe that all sentient beings should be blessed and not be made miserable.'

Because of this personality, it is natural that he harbors a deep hatred for devilish arts. To be precise, it is an aversion to unethical acts of sacrificing others for selfish reasons and to anything that is based on such actions, and for this reason, he refuses without any compromise or room for actions that involve the sacrifice of others, such as the cultivation pills. This became more in-depth after the 10th Cycle and was sublimated into a kind of identity.

Detailed Relations

Kim Young-hoon: A pioneer of martial arts and Seo Eun-hyun's teacher. In fact, they were not that close friends at work, but after flying to another world together, they developed into sworn brothers through the process of sharing life, death, and ups and downs. One of the precious colleagues who moved forward together with Seo Eun-hyun, sharing the position of struggling under fate despite possessing transcendental martial skills to the point of being called the incarnation of Heavenly Martial Arts. Young-hoon is Eun-hyun's first teacher and the one who led him down the path of Martial Arts.

Kang Min-hee: On Earth, Kang Min-hee and Seo Eun-hyun's relationship was described as 'incredibly awkward.' It was also revealed later that Kang Min-hee and Seo Eun-hyun have dated in the past, and were incredibly close.

Buk Hyang-hwa: Eun-hyun first met Buk Hwang-hwa in the Heaven Treading Desert... She was the first woman Seo Eun-hyun loved with all his heart and soul...

Kim Yeon: On Earth, Kim Yeon fell in love with Eun-hyun. Eun-hyun was too dense to notice it... Afterwards, in the 14th Cycle, he appears to acknowledge her as his significant other...

Jeon Myeong-hoon: On Earth, Myeong-hoon disliked Eun-hyun... In the 19th Cycle, Jeon Myeong-hoon joined the Wuji Religious Order under Seo Eun-hyun.

Oh Hyun-seok: On Earth, Hyun-seok was a person Eun-hyun could confide in... In the 19th Cycle, Oh Hyun-seok joined the Wuji Religious Order under Seo Eun-hyun.

Oh Hye-seo: She has continuously attempted to oppose Seo Eun-hyun throughout the Cycles... but she eventually submits to him in the 2010th Cycle.
