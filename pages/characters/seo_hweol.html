Seo Hweol

Sea Dragon King

"Hoho, greetings, Daoist Seo..."
—Seo Hweol, to Seo Eun-hyun, right after the former's death
He is the main antagonist of the Middle Realms. Seo Hweol was the personification of Tainted Soul Filling the Heavens and an innate Sacred Vessel.

Overview

Seo Hweol was the personification of Tainted Soul Filling the Heavens and an innate Sacred Vessel.

He was created through Ja Eum's Purple Soul Filling the Heavens, but his essence was corrupted through Agate's Hatred Dream.

He killed himself when he realised his 'origin' was Oh Hye-seo, the only being who had given him their heart, and passed on all that he was to her.

Appearance

Seo Hweol was described as a handsome middle-aged man or a handsome young man, having deer-like horns and either a top-knot or open blue hair. He always wore a gentle smile on his face, and was fond of laughing with a "hohoho". He usually had a calm disposition around him.

Personality

Outwardly Seo Hweol is the Sea Dragon King loved and respected by all, wearing a gentle smile in whatever situation may arise, when the ascendants had a conflict, just his words were enough to stop them, whom (aside from Mad Lord), all came to pay respect to him.

However, such personality is merely a façade. According to the Mad Lord, Seo Hweol's heart is dead, with him hiding behind a thick mask of lies and deceptions.

His true nature was seen after Eun-hyun glimpsed at his heart essence:

"Beyond heartless and shameless, his heart essence seems like a monster wearing a human mask.

Evil (惡).

He is the embodiment of endless malice."

After he successfully assimilated Seo Ran, Seo Hweol could only feel endless Hatred. However, after Oh Hye-seo accepted all of him, he realized he had denied his own existence by denying the heart. He killed himself for Oh Hye-seo's sake, passing on all of his "past" and knowledge to her.

However, later on the 19th cycle, after being assimilated by Seo Hweol, Eun-hyun could confirm that once in the past, his heart essence was originally a idyllic peach garden.
