Swallowing Heaven Supreme Deity (Hyeon Ryul), also known as The Immortal Beast Supreme Tiger, is an Innate Supreme Deity. True to his name, his true form is that of a black hole capable of consuming multiple superclusters. He rules the Twin Holding Heavenly Domain.

Overview

The Swallowing Heavens Supreme Deity is a Mantra left behind by the Obsidian Devil Heavenly King. Being an Innate Supreme Deity, he holds no Dao but is instead composed of the Flawless Mantra.

He governs over the realm of Integration. His physical form is a massive black hole, and his stomach is considered the safest place against [Light]. However, entry and exit require the Flawless Mantra. His internal world connects to various realms, including the deep Underworld, Outer Sea, and the Inner Sanctum of Radiance Hall.
