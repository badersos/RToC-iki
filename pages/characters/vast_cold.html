Vast Cold

The Strongest Ender

"Immortal Cultivation is progress. It's about being better today than you were yesterday. We are all always moving forward in some direction. Sometimes toward admiration, sometimes toward gratitude, sometimes toward affection, sometimes toward obsession, and sometimes toward sympathy."
Vast Cold is an Ender who is an important figure in the history of the Bright Cold Realm and Mount Sumeru as a whole. They are the former Ender of Love who twisted the title to contain "Vast Cold".

Overview

Yang Su-jin recognizes them as the strongest Ender. In the 21st regression, Seo Hweol reveals Vast Cold is still alive; their body (Bright Cold Realm) is simply in a vegetative state.

They twisted the conditions for The End of a Heavenly Domain from happening at the will of the Supreme Deity to happening when the living beings inside the Domain's Hearts reached their peak.

500,000 years ago, Vast Cold Heavenly Lord was capable of holding their ground in a battle against all the Heavenly Venerables and almost annihilated the Owner of Light.

Historical Remnants

Bright Cold Realm

The Bright Cold realm used to be called Vast Cold Realm. When its dragon ancestor, the Lofty Dragon True Person, died in the Blood Yin Great War, the realm was left unprotected and renamed to improve its fate.

Vast Cold Jade

In the 14th cycle, during the Human-Devil Great War, the invading Human Race used Vast Cold Jades to convert the devil veins of True Devil Realm into spirit veins.

In and Yeon

Vast Cold left behind a pair of beings fated to be named after Cause (In/因) and Connection (Yeon/緣). These beings reincarnate endlessly, born near each other, fall in love, and are fated to die together. Blessing their marriage grants a novel formula for Grand Perfection Heavenly Being based on the Merits of the Four Seasons.

Vast Cold Formula

A generic Earth Tribe cultivation method that serves as the basics of basics, universally applicable for everyone. It shares the name of Vast Cold.

Vast Cold Oath

Also known as the Vast Cold Beautiful Arrangement, it is an ancient marriage oath that binds a couple and grants them a fate to die together. It is cast through a hand seal and a kiss.

Trivia

  • Vast Cold was powerful enough to deal with Upper Immortals while at the level of Entering Nirvana.
  • Vast Cold was temporary allies with the Great Mountain Supreme Deity and received help from them when they ascended to True Immortality.
  • Vast Cold’s guide couldn’t understand him and ran away.
  • Vast Cold's name was meant to be Jesus but Tremendous saw it to be insensitive and inconsistent with the world-building so changed to Judas but then finally settled on Ahasuerus.
