Yang Su-Jin

Golden Immortal

"Right. You know well. The essence is ‘freedom’. Only beings that possess ‘freedom’ or have the potential to acquire ‘freedom’ are [Human] … Therefore, only we Enders are [Humans], and all other beings in this world, including True Immortals, are [Non-Humans]!"
Yang Su-Jin is an Ender, or transmigrator, from Earth. He founded the Golden Divine Heavenly Thunder Sect 123,000 years before Seo Eun-hyun's transmigration.

Overview

Yang Su-Jin ascended to the Middle Realm and eventually rose to the True Immortal realm. He is the most recent True Immortal to ascend. He was confirmed by Bong Hwa to be one of the Weaker Enders.

Goals and Motivations

Yang Su-Jin’s ultimate goal was freedom through defying fate, believing that Enders were the only ones capable of doing so as Earth is a reality without fate. However, he carelessly spoke out his fate, and in his final battle against ■■■■, he realized that Enders are also nothing more than non-humans themselves.

His final act was leaving behind fragments of himself in the Head Realm and Middle Realm, hoping later generations of Enders could carry on his legacy and gain freedom, while warning them never to mention their fate.

Trivia

  • Yang Su-Jin seems to have an extreme fascination with soft, jade white feet. Seo Eun-hyun believes this to be the primary reason he captured Zhengli, and also explains the human form of Golden Shaking Bird.
  • In a Q/A, the Author denied having a foot fetish but hinted otherwise regarding Yang Su-Jin and Zhengli.
  • Yang Su-Jin’s guide was Zhengli.
