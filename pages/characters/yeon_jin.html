Yeon Jin

Penglai Palace Lord · Disciple of Heaven

"The descendant who shared his body with the ancestor."
Yeon Jin (also known as Hon Jin) is a former disciple of the Golden Divine Heavenly Thunder Sect and is a descendant of Yeon Wei. He was a friend and fellow disciple of Seo Eun-hyun and Jeon Myeong-hoon and shared his body with his ancestor before joining the Wuji Religious Order.

Overview

After the events in the 998th and 99th Cycle, he inherited the position of Penglai Palace Lord from Hon Won. He learned the Great Mountain Splitting Emperor technique from a newly ascended Seo Eun-hyun, and through his own efforts attained the 'Supervisory Eye' given to Hon Won by Gwak Am.

He once considered pursuing Hong Fan if he had transformed into a girl, making him seem more outrageous than Ham Jin to Seo Eun-hyun.

Prowess

Integrated Dao Domain: Island of Taiji Great Dust Within Fierce Winds (太極大塵居烈風島). He compresses his Dao Domain into a single slash launched at his opponent.

Peak Cultivation: Quasi Star-Shattering.
