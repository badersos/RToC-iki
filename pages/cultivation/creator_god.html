Creator God

A Creator God is both a type of being and a realm that can be attained.

Sub Realms

0

Lifespan

Immortal

Description

In the first case, it refers to beings of pure effect and no cause, who are simply born from chaos and possess immense power. At some point, these beings create their own world, over which they have total control, and therefore they are omnipotent and omniscient within it.

For them, death is merely a change of state, because regardless of anything, they will eventually resurrect.

Every Creator God possesses something called a Divine Art, which is an innate ability.

In the second case, it refers to the position of a Creator God, which a being can reach by awakening their own Divine Art and accumulating vast power.

A being who reaches this position is also omnipotent and omniscient within their World.

All Creator Gods, including those who attained the position, are equal in power; they simply have different specializations.
