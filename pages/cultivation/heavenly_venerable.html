Heavenly Venerable

Heavenly Venerable is the sixth and final stage of the Great Boundary. It is preceded by Supreme Deity and succeeded by True Emperor.

Boundary

Great Boundary

Sub Realms

0

Lifespan

Immortal

Description

Heavenly Venerables are beings who have transcended life and death; they are not attached to the base of Sumeru, and instead, their Gandhara (True Body) are on the exterior.

The Heavenly Venerables are essentially unkillable, unless you manage to get them to drag their Gandhara (True Body) from the outside of Mount Sumeru, where they sit around the midpoint of the world. However, even the Heavenly Venerables will need a ritual to bring their true bodies into Sumeru.

Advancement

To become a Heavenly Venerable, a Supreme Deity must divine descend the power of one of the three Absolutes; Fate, History, or the [Third Absolute].

Trivia

  • The Gandhara of the Heavenly Venerables of Time, Void and Sal Tree are mediums of prophecy used by the Future King.
