Holders of the Seats of Origin

Immortal Lord

Immortal Lord is the fourth realm of the Greater Boundary. It is followed by the Supreme Deity realm.

Boundary

Greater Boundary

Sub Realms

Unknown

Lifespan

Immortal

Description

Immortal Lords are beings who have completely taken control of their [path], the wind they ride, and have become significantly free from attraction force. These are the beings closest to the [sky]. An Immortal Lord of the Fate pathway is called Heavenly Lord. An Immortal Lord of the Earth pathway is called a True Lord. A Dual Cultivation Immortal Lord is called an Origin Lord.

There are countless laws and principles, or Daos, in this world, and there is a place that can be called their origin. That is what we call Seats. The Seat is a symbol of a law, but at the same time, it is a sacred sanctuary (聖所). Only those who enter this sanctuary can be called Seat Holders, or Immortal Lords.

Beings under the path of a Supreme Deity may easily reach the realm of Great Net Immortal, but they can never become Seat Holders, in other words, Immortal Lords, as only one being can hold a seat at a time, excluding seats previously occupied by True Emperors.

The sanctuary of origin is not a place anyone can enter. Only those who completely seize the Seat, turning the Origin Source of the law into their own Audience Chamber and Throne are those who transcend the limits and boundaries of True Immortality to become Supreme Deities.

Advancement

The advancement to Immortal Lord is as follows:

  1. Use the Immortal Lord's realm formula, Yin-Yang and Five Elements Returning to One, to feel the power of Radiance and to fill the Origin Essence with their own [light], or [wisdom], thereby filling the whole Origin Essence.
  2. Either breathe the light and wisdom formed by your interpretation into an emptied Seat, or with that light and wisdom push away or kill the Immortal Lord, or Supreme Deity, who sits in that place. [1]
