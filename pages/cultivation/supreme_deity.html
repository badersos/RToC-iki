Supreme Deity

The Pinnacle of Immortal Cultivation

Supreme Deity

Supreme Deity is the fifth realm of the Greater Boundary, and the penultimate realm of immortal cultivation.

Boundary

Greater Boundary

Sub Realms

0

Lifespan

Immortal

Description

Supreme Deities are the beings at the pinnacle of immortal cultivation, whos very presence makes cultivation easier for lesser beings. Viewing the structure of Mount Sumeru you will see the seven Supreme Deities sat upon the flat base of Sumeru's inverted cone, watching over the entire world.

Supreme Deity can be achieved by grasping complete control over your Immortal Dao, allowing you to manipulate your seat and interpretation of it as you see fit.

Because the rank and authority of Supreme Deities have grown far too vast, an ordinary Immortal Title cannot contain their authority and rank. Therefore their True Titles are far longer than normal Immortals and their shortened names. For example, the Red Summit Great Mountain Life Phenomena Extinguishing Great Emperor and Great Mountain Supreme Deity titles both belonging to Gwak Am. As Supreme Deity's are already too complete of beings, their True Titles are used for debt enforcement upon those who recieved grace from their domain.

Advancement

The Origin essences are as massive as universes themselves. Because of this, if an Immortal Lord connects with and draws power from one, their Ego begins to erode. The way to become a Supreme Deity is to surpass the Origin Essence and stop the Ego erosion.

One must also face a Supreme Deity in battle at least once as an Immortal Lord to undersand what kind of being a Supreme Deity is. It is possible to advance without doing this, but it is nearly impossible.
