The Three Absolutes

True Emperor

True Emperor is the first stage after the Great Boundary. It is preceded by Heavenly Venerable and succeeded by Creator God.

Sub Realms

0

Lifespan

Immortal

Description

In total, three True Emperors exist within Mount Sumeru, corresponding to the Three Absolutes. They are:

  • The Future King Fate Supreme Deity
  • The First King Life Supreme Deity
  • The Crystal King Ender Supreme Deity

Advancement

To become a True Emperor, a Supreme Deity or Heavenly Venerable Beings must successfully take control of one the Absolute Laws of Sumeru.

To become the owner of an Absolute, you need 'qualification' and 'achievement'. Qualification involves becoming the person who understands that Absolute best, while achievement is gaining feats so great that they surpass that of the Absolute. An already existing True Emperor cannot influence the ascension of a new True Emperor. However, there are several ways to get around this rule, such as when the Future King had his subordinates kill the beings with the oldest histories of Mount Sumeru, thus making it easier for Bong Hwa to advance to Life Supreme Deity.
