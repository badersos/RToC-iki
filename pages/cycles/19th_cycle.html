Wuji Cult

19th Cycle

Throughout this cycle, light dims and brightens, and Seo Eun-hyun occasionally feels a ‘gaze’. He also notices Old Men Carrying Flower Baskets.
This cycle covers Chapter 326 to Chapter 410.

Axis

Seo Eun-hyun finds himself in the process of regression, thanks to Seo Li’s expertise of Ghost Path Methods and his own Four-Axis cultivation. He is in a [River]. He sees his soul has completely merged with the Colourless Glass Swords, the Soul Bearing Flower and Clear Scale Armour. The Axis of Wealth also follows. He looks up to see the [Heavens]. He recognises ten [seats]; four central stars with [rings], and six outer seats with only one having a ring. As he looks above, he realises the [Oldest One] has sensed his gaze and is now chasing him through the regression process. The Clear Scale Armour increases the attraction force between Seo Eun-hyun and the [Oldest One], so he desperately rips it out of his soul and uses the Soul Bearing Flower. This momentarily block the Owner of the Netherworld, but invites the ire of the original owner of the Soul-Bearing Flower. Seo Eun-hyun successfully escapes and begins his 19th regression.

Seo Eun-hyun returns to the body of the Vestige. He identifies the third attraction force as Ancient Force. He immediately breaks through to Four-Axis with Jeon Myeong-hoon holding off the Tribulation. Hon Won appears and they fight. Hon Won says he will let them go if they endure one strike. Seo Eun-hyun transforms into a giant of starlight with nineteen heads. Both of them use Great Mountain Splitting Emperor. Seo Eun-hyun sees the name ‘Cheon Ra’ within Hon Won, and the latter sees an inverted cone. Mountains begin growing on Seo Eun-hyun and Hon Won begins turning into a salt pillar. The latter recovers first and begins another strike, but is hit by Yuan Yu. Hon Won is weakened. Seo Eun-hyun ponders why he was not severely affected by the name Cheon Ra, even though he’s sure it belongs to one of the ten [seats]. Hon Won’s Supervisory Eye seizes up and he is forced to let Seo Eun-hyun go.

As Seo Eun-hyun, Myeong-hoon and Hong Fan travel, they are accosted by Seo Hweol. Hweol is surprised at the absence of Tainted Soul Filling the Heavens within Seo Eun-hyun. He once again pretends to be a True Immortal, trying to covertly imprint himself into Seo Eun-hyun. Seo Eun-hyun begins dropping names of Reapers from the Netherworld, taunting Hweol’s claim of being Blood Yin. Hweol attempts to brainwash Seo Eun-hyun by sacrificing personalities, but the Karmic Fire spreads to him instead. Hweol quickly severs the connection, but Seo Eun-hyun reconnects using the All-Heavens Sword. He chases Hweol and fills his ears with [knowledge] while scanning for his secrets. He discovers Hweol has a main body. Seo Hweol self-destructs and escapes.

Seo Eun-hyun, Myeong-hoon and Hong Fan go to the remains of the Golden Divine Heavenly Thunder Sect and pay their respects. He plans to rescue all his companions, and has Hong Fan teach Myeong-hoon the Six Extremes Yin Thunder Body. Seo Eun-hyun goes to the Grand Nirvana Temple and is shocked to see it ruined. Tael Yeol-jeon has been replaced by a nameless “nun”. This nun’s personality is nothing like that of Yeol-jeon. Inside the Palace, Seo Eun-hyun finds a book detailing the same fairy tales he read in the Head Realm. He remembers an extra chapter, but not its content. He buries his worries for the moment and leaves. He rises to Grand Perfection Heavenly Being in Heaven and discovers his Nascent Soul now acts like an Axis.

Old Monster’s Rampage

Seo Eun-hyun goes to Human Heavenly Island and fights for the Position of Grand Cultivator. During the fight, he notices an old man carrying a flower basket. He is reluctantly accepted as a Grand Cultivator.

Seo Eun-hyun takes Hong Fan to the unnamed monk. Hong Fan does not show any attraction to her this time around. They then go to rescue Kim Yeon. Seo Eun-hyun fights the Mad Lord and attempts to cure him, but discovers the Madness is incurable. Mad Lord momentarily regains his sanity, and gives Kim Yeon to Seo Eun-hyun. He also gives her a magic artifact containing his teachings.

Seo Hweol asks Oh Hye-seo to track Seo Eun-hyun’s history. Hye-seo gives him the information she had collected about him on Earth. Hweol tells her Seo Eun-hyun has been taken over by a high-ranking entity. Hye-seo uses her talent to scan Seo Eun-hyun’s past and discovers he is the same as always. Hyeo-seo plays with the idea of turning into a double agent.

Kim Yeon observes Seo Eun-hyun’s intent and finds he has the same emotions towards Kang Min-hee as he has towards her– that of an ex-girlfriend. Seo Eun-hyun realises he does not love the current Kim Yeon. Kim Yeon tells Seo Eun-hyun she will persist in loving Seo Eun-hyun until he loves her back.

They join Myeong-hoon at the entrance to the True Devil Realm. Oh Hyun-seok has fallen to Core Formation after being injured. Seo Eun-hyun invites Hyun-seok to join them. They then go to Black Ghost Valley to meet Kang Min-hee. Outside Black Ghost Valley, Seo Eun-hyun sees a projection of Hye-seo invisible to the others. Hye-seo uses her powers to create a formation with the Valley unnoticed.

Heo Gwak and Heo Ryeong take Seo Eun-hyun into the sect grounds and bring Min-hee’s clone to him. He and Min-hee are aware of Hye-seo. Since no one else can sense her, Min-hee’s main body is summoned. A Tainted Soul Filling the Heavens clone of Hye-seo emerges from Seo Eun-hyun’s shadow. Seo Hweol uses the configurations in Heo Gwak and Heo Ryeong along with Hye-seo’s powers to trap all of them in a pure white labyrinth.

Hye-seo continues thinking about her double-agent plan. Seo Eun-hyun breaks into the part of the space where Hye-seo and Hweol are and captures Hye-seo. Hweol leaves her behind. Hye-seo pretends to be under Hweol’s influence and attacks Seo Eun-hyun. She begs for death, which Seo Eun-hyun gladly complies with. She flees immediately. Seo Eun-hyun eventually catches her and uses her to manipulate the space, bringing all his comrades to him. He begins questioning her, but Hweol emerges from her shadow to retrieve her soul. He attempts to send the group to the Blood Yin Realm. Hweol sees Kang Min-hee’s absurd talent and attempts to retrieve her. Inside her heart essence, he sees the Netherworld’s right hand man, the Chief Judge, judges him. He begins the descent into Min-hee. Seo Eun-hyun attempts to use Extinguishing Divine Tribulating Heavens to sever the colossal being’s connection. The entity blinds Seo Hweol, making it such that none of his configurations can see, and takes the calamity from Extinguishing Divine Tribulating Heavens upon itself. Seo Eun-hyun attempts to stop Min-hee’s Ghost Guiding Holy Mother Transformation, but she pushes all of them into the Nether Ghost Realm.

The Deceased

The group summons Yeon Wei from within Yeon Jin. She tells them they are in the Nether Ghost Realm and warns them from observing the celestial energy in the day, as Entering Nirvana True Persons are always observing. Seo Eun-hyun plans to build the Longevity Axis in Nether Ghost. Yeon Wei tells him how to build a Blessings Axes; spread your influence, descend to lower realm through prayers, and exchange lifespan for favours. Accumulating ten-thousand years of lifespan grants an Axis.

Seo Eun-hyun visits the nearby Black River City for information. All the ghosts in the city are too scared to answer his questions. He also notices an old man with a flower basket. Dumbfounded, he finds the city lord, a ghost called Man Gol. Man Gol explains him having a living body and a profound aura of death implies he is a monster who possibly sacrificed millions of souls. As death is suppressed in the daytime, none of the ghosts actually fled on sight. Night suddenly falls. Seo Eun-hyun’s death aura is revealed and the entire city flees.

Yeon Wei tells them they have landed in the White Yin Domain. There is a Void Spirit Pond,which is one of the requirements for building a Five Blessing Axis, nearby. They conquer the Pond and name themselves the Wuji Religious Order, with Seo Eun-hyun as the cult’s head. The cult offers puppet bodies and welfare benefits for the ghosts.

Wei Shi-hon of the Death Corpse Sect, Eum Wa of the Shadow Yin Gate and Baek Rin of the White Vein Sect, hold an emergency meeting. Their shared Void Spirit Pond has been captured by the Wuji Religious Order. Shi-hon aggressively pushes for an attack. They lay out all the information they have on the cult, after Shi-hon proposes an attack at night, when the Cult Leader is absent.

Seo Eun-hyun has begun forming his Axis. However, the act of taking lifespan still feels off to him. At the same time, the three Sects begin their attack.

Baek Rin is startled to see underlings of his own sect among the puppets. The puppet army of ghosts surrounds them. Shi-hon says he will hold the army back and tells the others to advance. Eun Wa and Baek Rin continue to the core of the cult and trapped in a formation laid by Kim Yeon.

Kim Yeon has been pondering the emotion she feels towards Seo Eun-hyun. She feels it isn’t simply love. She has been practicing her Pair Wings Dance and another method taught by Hong Fan. She keeps Eum Wa and Baek Rin trapped, but they eventually escape. They find a building entirely covered with curses and attempt to enter. However, they are blocked by Wi Shi-hon, who was actually a Guardian Ghost King of the Wuji Religious Order. Wi Shi-ho enticed them into invading the Order in order to convert them. They eventually overpower him and enter the building, only to rush out screaming. Kim Yeon manages to capture Eum Wa, but Baek Rin escapes thanks to her sacrifice.

Baek Rin’s escape worries Wei Shi-hon as he is owed a favour by an Elder of the Black Ghost Palace. Yeon Wei tells them not to worry as the White Yin Domain is a neutral zone.

Two hundred and thirty years pass. Oh Hyun-seok has reached the Grand Perfection Heavenly Being stage from the Nascent Soul stage, Jeon Myeong-hoon has reached Grand Perfection Four-Axis stage, and Hong Fan has reached the mid Four-Axis stage. Kim Yeon’s cultivation realm has remained stagnant while her martial arts have improved. Seo Eun-hyun has built 70% of his Axis. The Penglai Island, Underworld Ghost Palace, and Black Ghost Palace have designated the Wuji Religious Order as a heretical cult, and have declared the Cult Leader as the public enemy of the White Yin Domain.

Seo Eun-hyun has found the disciples of the Golden Divine Heavenly Thunder Sect in the region where corpses of the devotees of Entering Nirvana True Persons lie. He is also worried about Kim Yeon, who has been having bouts of madness. During her madness, her martial strength surges.

First Guardian Ghost King Wei Shi-hon and the Second Guardian Ghost King Eum Wa are getting married. They send a recording of their wedding to Baek Rin. Baek Rin, who is on the Nether Crossing Ship of Blue Grand Elder, Cha Jo-gwi, goes crazy upon seeing this recording and begs Jo-gwi to annihilate the cult. As Baek Rin is prepared to do anything, Jo-gwi agrees.

All three cults move their forces to attack the White Yin Domain. Seo Eun-hyun gives a speech.

Devilish Cult

The three sects begin their attack. Seo Eun-hyun uses Great Desert to Dead Sea to absorb all their attacks to power a flying/teleportation Realm Annihilation Ground-Shrinking Formation they have engraved on the White Yin Domain, which is now renamed to Twilight Domain. Myeong-hoon recaptures Baek Rin, also capturing several other ghosts, including Cha Jo-gwi's lieutenant and lover, Yu Hye. Yu Hye has the others escape. In the end, only she and Baek Rin are captured.

Seo Eun-hyun finds himself summoned to the lower realms again. He finds himself summoned by Ham Jin, an orphan being experimented on by a devilish cultivator. Seo Eun-hyun realises the valley he is in was created by a creature killed by Jang Ik. He investigates a little, and grants Ham Jin the vision of the Earth Tribe and withdraws.

The Twilight Domain is again attacked. Oh Hyun-seok begins and advancement to Four-Axis and Myeon-hoon uses the Tribulation to defend the Domain. They teleport once more.

Yu Hye is now friends with the order. She asks for a transmission device to reassure Cha Jo-gwi she is safe. However, the message distorts due to the teleportation, and make Jo-gwi believe she has been brainwashed and assimilated into the cult, driving him mad with grief.

The Twilight Domain arrives near the place the disciples of the Golden Divine Heavenly Thunder Sect are. The land is covered with corpses of ‘devotees’ of True Persons. These ‘devotees’ are ghostly creatures who observed the celestial energy during the day and beheld the True Person, being corrupted. Seo Eun-hyun, Myeong-hoon and Hyun-seok explore the area, looking for the disciples.

A month later, just as they find the disciples, Seo Eun-hyun has his consciousness pulled up by the True Persons observing Nether Ghost. The True Persons wish to converse with the entity backing Seo Eun-hyun. They treat Seo Eun-hyun like an insect. Infuriated, Seo Eun-hyun uses the Great Mountain Splitting Emperor Technique to show them the [inverted cone]. He confronts them with the fact they have been twisting fate and distorting information about the cult. The True Persons, severely affected by [knowledge], inadvertently lower the pressure on Seo Eun-hyun. Seo Eun-hyun names Reincarnation Judge of the Netherworld, Nether Ghost True Lord Yu Soo Ryeon, whose death formed the Nether Ghost Realm, and returns to his body.

On their return to the Order, Seo Eun-hyun warns his followers about the threat of the True Persons, but his followers pledge loyalty to him anyway.

Ham Jin prays to the Wuji Ghost King again. Ham Jin informs Seo Eun-hyun about his master wanting his body, and Seo Eun-hyun reveals his goals for something underneath the valley. He promises to raise Ham Jin to Qi Building within the year. He begins teaching him the Silica Earth Great Wall Secrets.

Ham Jin’s master, Black Tomb Old Monster Yeom Gok is disappointed as yet another one of his disciples has died in his experiments. He feels as if Ham Jin is changing and wonders if it is because of the power at the bottom of the valley.

Seo Eun-hyun wonders if simply giving ten thousand years of lifespan to the Nether Ghost Realm would grant him an Axis.

The Order prepares to fight the forces of the Nether Ghost Realm. Myeong-hoon worries about Yeon as her cycles of madness have been growing shorter.

Ham Jin breaks into the Qi Building stage, thanks to the tutoring of Sir Infinite Fighting Ghost, a subordinate of the Wuji Ghost King. Yeom Gok appears overjoyed and tells him he will ‘give him everything’ in three days. He also invites the other Core Formation Sage Gods, to witness his succession (possession). Fighting Ghost tells Ham Jin not to worry and disappears.

The forces of the Nether Ghost realm begin their attack. Myeong-hoon begins his advancement to Integration stage and confronts them within his Heavenly Tribulation.

Seo Eun-hyun has finished gathering lifespans, but feels uneasy looking at the Axis made of others lifespan. He ignores this feeling and condensed his Longevity Axis. Myeong-hoon senses this and delays the Tribulation. Seo Eun-hyun comes out and faces the ‘devotees’ of the True Persons. Myeong-hoon defends the Order while Seo Eun-hyun focuses on opening a sidepath to Bright Cold. The True Persons descend via their devotees and postpone the Heavenly Tribulation. They attempt to crush the Twilight Domain through sheer pressure.

Seo Eun-hyun, despairing, decides to return all the lifespan he has collected over the years. He rips apart the Axis. A [voice] asks him if it isn’t a waste, to which he says it doesn’t matter. The voice commends him and rips apart seven thousand years Seo Eun-hyun had and hands it to the remains of the Longevity Axis. Seo Eun-hyun realises “Immortal Cultivation is repentant enlightenment”. The [voice] agrees and proceeds to tell Seo Eun-hyun the complete formula of the technique, from which the Great Mountain Splitting Emperor is derived. Seo Eun-hyun uses the nameless formula to display an [inverted cone], a mountain of salt. He cuts off the connection the True Persons had with their devotees, and rips open a side path. The Twilight Domain leaves, towards the Bright Cold Realm.

Is It Not A Joy To Have Friends Come From Afar?

The Twilight Domain traverses the Void with Jeon Myeong-hoon at the wheel. Suddenly, Seo Eun-hyun’s Colourless Glass Swords [resonate] with something in the Void and the Twilight Domain is suddenly pushed into the Ancient Force Realm.

Yuk Yo, a koi demon beast, has partially transformed into a Sea Dragon and is joyfully scamming people, when she suddenly sees a vast island appear in the sky. She is caught by the people of the island and asked if she knows Seo Ran.

Seo Eun-hyun has captures a koi demon bearing the appearance of Seo Ran, The Koi demon, Yuk Yo, confesses to having bedded Seo Ran to steal his appearance, but was caught and chased by Seo Ran’s wife. Seo Eun-hyun has Yuk Yo explain the structure of the Ancient Force Realm. After that, she is led to the honoured guest chamber. Meanwhile, Hong Fan has found and brought Seo Ran and his “wife”, Shi Ho, the white fox from Ascension Path.

An old ghost and a woman with a norigae attempt to track Seo Ran down. They are terrified when he appears to be within the belly of the being that crossed over from the Nether Ghost Realm.

Shi Ho smells Yuk Yo’s scent and leaves to assault her. Seo Ran has Seo Eun-hyun erect a barrier and confesses to the fox being male. Seo Eun-hyun finds he cannot say anything to the two men, and wishes them a beautiful love. They share information about Seo Hweol and Cheongmnun Ryeong’s situation. Seo Ran shows Seo Eun-hyun items from the Ancient Force Realm, including a Supervisory Jade. He uses this jade to visit Ham Jin.

Ham Jin has been worried ever since Sir Infinite Fighting Ghost vanished. The Three Sage Gods of Yuhwa are preparing for the Devil God Black Tomb Old Devil’s inheritance ceremony. During the ceremony, Black Tomb betrays the other two, but is reduced to a Golden Core. The two Sage Gods attack Ham Jin, who manages to hold his own ground until Black Tomb creates a ghost king body and kills them. He takes Ham Jin and Fire Merchant’s body underground. At the bottom of the valley lies a Spirit Jade. Black Tomb possesses the body and implants the spirit jade within himself. Ham Jin suddenly notices his shadow has nineteen heads, which activates a formation Ham Jin had been laying out in the valley on orders of the Wuji Ghost King. Black Tomb attempts to kill Ham Jin, but feels a greater threat. He sees a man in white. The valley suddenly collapses. Black Tomb has Ham Jin drive a flying artifact out of the valley. He starts seeing a hideous ghost king following them.The Wuji Ghost King emerges from Ham Jin’s Inner Core, and kills Black Tomb, seizing the spirit jade.

Seo Eun-hyun examines the formations and finds a droplet-like world. He then withdraws his awareness. Near the Twilight Domain, subordinates of Yuk Yo’s father, the Governing Dragon Palace Lord Yuk Rin. They attack with no warning. En-hyun kills them all, but allows their souls to escape.

Yuk Rin organises a mission to “rescue” his daughter. A man in a black robe and wearing a bamboo hat also joins. The Twilight Domain is attacked by the Conqueror King’s Fleet. Seo Eun-hyun and Kim Young-hoon have a spar. The Conqueror King, Buk Hyang-hwa, fights with Kim Yeon (waifu wars). Kim Yeon defeats Hyang-hwa, but Seo Eun-hyun, who won the spar with Young-hoon while destroying the laters entire sword collection, stops her from making a General Buk. He then uses the nameless mantra, which resonates with the realm of Ancient Force, and kills all the other warriors. Kim Yeon notices a suspicious bit of intent directed from Seo Eun-hyun to Hyang-hwa.

Hyang-hwa wakes up to find Kim Yeon stroking her cheek. She takes her to the meeting hall. The Conqueror’s fleet and Wuji Religious Hall hammer out an alliance. Hyang-hwa feels a strange sadness after meeting Seo Eun-hyun. She and Kim Yeon bond over it. The Wuji Religious Order are contacted by Yuk Rin with the proposal of an alliance.

Holding Hands

They head to Governing Dragon Island. During their talks, Yuk Rin reveals he knows the location of a Deep Sea Island. Yuk Rin produces a contract written on Black Ancient Paper, which cannot be broken without severe backlash. In the middle of the explanation of the Island, Yuk Rin is forced to go out to retrieve Yuk Yo again. Seo Eun-hyun and Kim Young-hoon discuss the Salt Mountain Yuk Rin showed them. The Wuji Religious Order takes over Yuk Rin’s palace and takes him prisoner. The ghosts begin redevelopment of the land around them. Due to their appearance, a half-fish half-human demon beast called Wi Yun falls into the Sea.

Yuk Rin has recognised Yeon Wei as the Betrayal King. He tells Seo Eun-hyun the Black Ancient Paper is powerless in the Deep Sea Island, Penglai Palace. The Contract on the Paper is changed to a slave contract.

Wi Yun finds herself in a dark place with the statue of a Sea Dragon.

Seo Eun-hyun and Young-hoon discuss their martial art’s enlightenment. Young-hoon tells him it is impossible for him to reach the Second Step Before the Throne, as it involves severing all connections. They have a small duel, in which Young-hoon reveals the name of his First Step Before the Throne; Seated Detachment, Standing Oblivion. He returns to being beaten up by Oh Hyun-seok.

The Twilight Domain reaches the end of the Sea Domain. Yeon Wei assists Seo Eun-hyun in breaking through towards another Domain. They find themselves in a middle zone, which seems similar to Ham Jin’s home planet. They enter the Black Sea Domain, where most of the Buk Hyang fleet is located. Seo Eun-hyun goes and possesses Ham Jin to use the spirit jade. While traveling on the path to the Lightning Sacred Sea, he comes across Jang Ik. They have a spar, after which Jang Ik takes him in as his 23rd disciple.

At the Sea

When Seo Eun-hyun comes to his senses, he finds himself in a hot spring. He exits it to find himself surrounded by Esteemed Ones. Jang Ik ‘rescues’ him, and they discuss the enlightenment of the Second Step. He also tells Seo Eun-hyun the universe will End in ten thousand years. Jang Ik tells Seo Eun-hyun to cut down a star in one stroke to reach the next realm. Seo Eun-hyun thanks him for his guidance, and leaves with Ham Jin. He returns to his main body and finds the cult has gone into a panic as he was unconscious. Yuk Yo and Baek Rin have been kidnapped, Kim Yeon and Buk Hyang-hwa were captured trying to rescue them.

Kim Yeon and Hyang-hwa begin modifying the Salt Bones Ship of the Fighting Ghost Race in a bid to escape. They are, however, stopped by Jin Ma-yeol and run with Baek Rin and Yuk Yo. during the rescue operation, Seo Eun-hyun sees a statue of a Sea Dragon, which he takes for investigation. Baek Rin and Yuk Yo flee, as bait. Hyang-hwa and Kim Yeon grow closer during the escape. Baek Rin, who has fallen in love with Yuk Yo, defends her from Ma-yeol. All four are rescued, but Hyang-hwa suffers a grievous wound during the escape. Seo Eun-hyun exterminates the Salt Bones Ship in response. He also discovers a ‘star’ of calamity bestowed upon him.

With Everyone

Seo Eun-hyun shows the statue to Seo Ran. He tells Seo Eun-hyun it is Lofty Dragon’s Seal (stamp). He discusses his martial arts with Young-hoon and decides to combine all of his techniques into one. Seo Eun-hyun implants Jang Ik’s podao within Young-hoon.

A hundred years pass. Song Jin has died peacefully. Seo Eun-hyun has realised he does not understand the Canvas. Seo Eun-hyun has condensed the Health axis. Seo Ran and Shi Ho have also advanced their realms. Yuk Rin leads them to Penglai Island. The Twilight Domain enters.

Seo Eun-hyun the charcoal seller wakes up from a long dream. He suddenly remembers his life as Seo Eun-hyun, Wuji Ghost King. He discovers all of his comrades have lost their memories. They all are living happy lives, but Seo Eun-hyun is considered a despicable man. He discovers the world is structured around their memories of folktales from Earth. He visits the forest demon, and discovers it is Shi Ho. He defeats him and goes to Shi Ho’s den, where he discovers Seo Ran, who has retained his memories.

Seo Eun-hyun is aware he must become the king of Penglai to exit the world. He begins gathering demon spirits and creates an army.

The Scion of the Jin Clan, Jin Ma-yeol, hears about the King of Demon Spirits creating an army. He is confined by his family, but escapes and decides to fight the King. He is joined in his quest by Princess Yuk Yo, Son of the Dragon King Yuk Rin, wandering scholar Kim Young-hoon and a shaman called Baek Rin. They confront the King of Demon Spirits, Seo Eun-hyun, but are defeated in battle and only Jin Ma-yeol and Yuk Rin manage to escape.

Salt

Yuk Yo tells Seo Eun-hyun she is originally of this world, and tells him she perceives his world as a dream, not hers. She was born to Yuk Rin and his wife in Penglai. Yuk Rin eventually convinced her and her siblings to exit the world, but once outside he extracted their Sea Dragon True Blood for his own ends. Yuk Yo was left alive as she did not have much blood. She also tells them the walking condition involves having many who cherish you.

Yuk Yo further tells them Yuk Rin has transferred his ‘main body’ to that of the old Palace Lord, Yuk Ung. Seo Eun-hyun wonders if Yuk Rin is using Tainted Soul Filling the Heavens.

Wi Yun has been increasing her power through blood sacrifices to the Sea Dragon statue. She receives new orders and leaves to fulfil them.

Seo Eun-hyun returns to the village, He discovers Yeon Wei has awakened and killed Hon Won out of desperation. Jeon Myeong-hoon is awake as well, but not yet crazy. Oh Hyun-seok, whom Seo Eun-hyun had captured, has awakened as well. They storm the palace and enter the throne room. The queen hand over her crown and seal to Seo Eun-hyun. However, before he can do anything, Yuk Rin activates Purple Soul Filling the Heavens in an attempt to take over Seo Eun-hyun body, cultivation, memories, and enlightenment. He barely guessed that it is a secret art that seizes the body, disassemble the memories, soul, abilities, and bloodline, absorbing the advantageous parts, and expelling what is disadvantageous. However, it is lukewarm compared to Tainted Soul Filling the Heavens which bears the name of an Immortal Art as it's rate of corruption is slow while Tainted Soul Filling the Heavens is swift and stealthy. Seo Eun-hyun is sure of something and that is both shares the same root, giving Seo Eun-hyun a clue to Tainted Soul Filling the Heavens.

Seo Eun-hyun invite Yuk Rin's mind into his heart essence. His body writhe and scream in pain as he is torn apart in Seo Eun-hyun Sword Mountain but he still managed to keep is mind intact. Seo Eun-hyun slowly climb up the mountain while inviting Yuk Rin to chase him if he wanted his body.

Yuk Rin does but he is inflicted with all of Seo Eun Hyun's "pain" but he managed to touch Seo Eun Hyun where the Karmic Fire burns him. He tries to get away from Seo Eun Hyun to escape the pain from the Karmic Fire but all of "Eun Hyun's corpses" grab at his body. He is unable to fight back and escapes from Seo's heart essence by self destructing his mental body. From this encounter Seo Eun Hyun is able to learn Purple Soul Filling the Heavens.

Yuk Rin returns to his body where he clutch his head in pain and gasp for his breath while groaning in pain and calling Seo Eun Hyun a charcoal monster. Seo Eun Hyun thinks that he briefly touched the Karmic Fire that only has a few embers left and says Yuk Rin is overexaggerating. Seo Eun-hyun starts interrogating him to find what his objective is. Seo Eun Hyun suddenly starts feeling strength from his sleeping body that is outside the formation. He stomps on Yuk Rin, questioning what he is plotting only to know that his plan already began.

Despite the fact that he couldn't take Eun Hyun's body, Yuk Rin laughs and says he achieved his objectives. It is then revealed that he used the half crazed Wi Yun to completely resurrect and control the former Cherry Blossom Dragon Palace Lord, Yuk Ung, and opening up Penglai Island to get the palace made of Salt Crystals at the summit of Salt Mountain. Seo Eun-hyun and Kim Young-hoon use the one strike of the First Step to create two Heart Avatars to fight against Yuk Rin. Yuk Rin is unable to defeat them even after taking control of Yuk Ung's body so he uses the Salt Sea Returning Dew Jade with the palace made of Salt Crystals to try to win. To this Seo counters using the nameless modified Great Mountain Splitting Emperor Technique (Phenomena Extinguishing Mantra), he is winning, but the crash of the techniques makes the Sacred Master, Hae Lin, intervene and push the Wuji Religious Order to the Deep Sea of Ancient Force to save them from Great Mountain's attention. Yuk Rin then betrays Jin Ma-Yeol and takes Yuk Yo before leaving.

Drink

After the palace made of Salt Crystals has been taken by Yuk Rin, the illusion formation collapsed and ritual worship scriptures and treasures that Seo Eun Hyun wanted is in the palace, meaning the Axis of Love of Virtue is out of reach.
