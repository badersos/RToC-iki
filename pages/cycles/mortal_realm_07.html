16th Regression

"Wandering for 500 years with Hong Fan to gather strength and escape the curse, But ultimately failing to completely escape the curse, dying after leaving a will in front of Hong Fan.

That was my seventeenth return."
— Seo Eun-hyun

Chapter 222, 781-???

Details

Not much is known about the sixteenth regression, except:

  • Eun-hyun believes it lasted five hundred years.
  • He remained with Hong Fan.
  • He only remembered "Gu Ju", which was presumably a name he gave to someone or a name he received.
  • Hong Fan achieved Entering Heavens Beyond the Path.
  • According to Kim Young-hoon this cycle must have been more than 20 billion years in reality, as he claims Eun-hyun is older than him, despite spending 20 billion years in the Outer Sea.
  • From chapter 683, the line "Judging by its depth, the history Seo Eun-hyun experienced in the 16th cycle must have been overwhelmingly immense..." supports this.
  • Seo Eun-hyun went to the Outer Sea through the Heavenly Void Furnace and made a promise with the Heavenly Venerable of Time.
  • Most likely ended with his memories sealed by a True Emperor.
