First Stage of Manifestation

Entering Heavens Beyond the Path

First Stage of Manifestation (월도입천/越道入天)

At this realm, one is capable of fighting those that have attained the equivalent of Core Formation in the Heaven/Earth tribe methods.

Description

The key to reaching the stage beyond this lies in finding the form of consciousness that best suits humans and the enlightenment of Entering Heavens Beyond The Path is unique for every individual. Each individual ponders on their own enlightenment of life and creates a unique manifestation derived from the experiences of that person. The manifestation made by that person takes the form of their deepest desire and converts it to reality. One example of this is Kim Young-Hoon's Surpassing Radiant Saber, where his desire to return to his family transformed into a Golden Peng bird capable of soaring into the sky.

The typical rules someone in this realm follows are as below:

  • The Gang Spheres melt into the Manifestation, and the Gang Spheres disappear.
  • Manifests a person's unique heart essence, usually by imbuing the heart essence (9 Gang Spheres you've achieved Ultimate Pinnacle) to their divine consciousness.
  • Achieved by combining the external and internal heart essences into one, through a longing beyond imagination.
