Martial Pinnacle (武極)

The Seventh and final stage of Manifestation. The pinnacle of martial arts.

Description

It is the practitioners answer to the strongest Martial Arts. It requires the wielder to master triple divinity, then cover the domain of purity with your own will through your martial arts.[5]

According to Hyeon Mu, you do this by scattering yourself through it and assimilating into the world; the method used to create the Plane of Soul, Vast Cold used to create their cultivation system and [someone] used to spread the 'Immortal Art Immortal Cultivation' in the world.

This stage has, in the entire history of Mount Sumeru, only ever been achieved by:

  • [Owner of Fate]
  • Hyeon Mu
  • Kim Young-hoon
  • Seo Eun-hyun

It is not something that can be achieved through time, effort or even putting your entire soul into it.[6] It is believed, by some, to only be achievable when your art approaches infinity.[7]

To reach this stage, it is necessary for the martial art to be recognized by the Future King.

Beings of this stage are capable of fighting against Supreme Deities and Heavenly Venerables.
