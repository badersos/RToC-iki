Peak Master (절정/絕頂)

Also known as the Pinnacle Realm.

Description

Begins to peer into the world of intent as two colors (red: opponent / blue: wielder). The distinctions of the realm are as follows:

  • Early: Acclimatize the brain to seeing intent, seeing either Red (others) or Blue (oneself) lines at a time.
  • Middle: See both Red and Blue lines simultaneously. Infuse intent into attacks (‘weapon’ Aura / Sword Silk) and become able to project far more lines.
  • Late: Discover Purple (the intersection of Red and Blue)

Martial artists in this realm are widely respected in the mortal worlds. They see another world from the world of first class, and it is said that at least 50 first class martial artists need to gather in order to exhaust and beat a Peak Master.
