Second Stage of Manifestation

Treading Heaven Beyond The Path

Second Stage of Manifestation (월도답천/越道踏天)

At this realm, one is able to fight practitioners that have strength equivalent to the Nascent Soul Stage.

Description

The typical rules someone of this realm follows are as below:

  • Fully integrates one's ideals to the self, by assimilating the 10th Gang Sphere (the inner dantian) into one's consciousness.
  • Casting aside thoughts of fate and putting one's heart into a state of emptiness (空).
  • Realizing the powers of ones manifestation and allowing it to flow freely through the body, similar to a Qi Building cultivator.
  • Lifespan increases by 49 years.
