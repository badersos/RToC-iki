Second Step Before The Throne

Void Shattering

Second Step Before The Throne

Also known as: Void Shattering (虛空分碎) (Kim Young-hoon), Void Shattering (虛空分碎) (Seo Eun-hyun), Prajna (般若) (Underworld faction), Mandala (曼陀羅) (Hyeon Mu).

Description

"One must forget.
In other words, one must sever.
Sever all attachments to one's past, all fears about one's fate.
Only by severing and forgetting everything, leaving only 'this moment' and reaching true Emptiness (空), can one get this step."

Only Jang-Ik, Seo Eun-hyun and Kim Young-hoon attained this step in the entire Bright Cold Realm. Beings at this stage can freely travel through the Plane of Soul.

Beings at this stage are capable of fighting Star Shattering and Sacred Vessel stage cultivators.
