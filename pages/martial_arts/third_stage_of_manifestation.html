Shattering Heavens Beyond the Path

Third Stage of Manifestation (월도쇄천/越道碎天)

Also known as Tribulating Heavens Beyond the Path (越道劫天). At this realm, one is able to fight practitioners that have strength equivalent to the Heavenly Being Stage.

Description

In the Bright Cold Realm, one can be considered an expert of the Heart Tribe once they have attained this realm. This is due to the fact that when practitioners at this realm attack, those who fail to overcome it gain an extra Heavenly Tribulation, which appears whenever they advance in cultivation and carries the same power as the original strike.[1][2]

The typical rules someone of this realm follows are as below:

  • One can create 100 Gang Spheres
  • Awakens the heart of one's martial arts, imbuing life upon it. One's martial arts is now its own living being, an avatar of its user as well as a form of Heavenly Tribulation that the user enforces upon the world.
  • Lifespan increases by 343 years.
  • When calling down Heavenly Tribulation, they negatively influence the fates of all those nearby.[1]
