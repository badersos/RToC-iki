Ultimate Pinnacle

A Unique Stage of Martial Arts

Ultimate Pinnacle (등봉조극/登峰造極)

This stage was exclusively created by Kim Young Hoon. In the history of the world, no one else has ever made this stage, and the only two other people that have attained this realm are Seo Eun Hyun and Hong Su-ryeong.

Characteristics

A master at the Ultimate Pinnacle has the following characteristics:

  • Divides the consciousness to create Gang Spheres, which can be used as clones made up of intent or accelerate one's own consciousness.
  • Can create an inner dantian that converges with Earth tribe methods. This is made to store all Internal Energy, and prevent there being any Heterogeneity with Spiritual Energy.
  • Can create up to 9 Gang Spheres corresponding to the interactions of Heaven, Earth and Man with themselves (3^2).
