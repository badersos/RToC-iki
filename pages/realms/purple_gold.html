Purple Gold Realm

자금 (紫金)

Overview

The Purple Gold Realm is one the five Middle Realms and is known for its artifacts and Dharma Treasure's[1] and is also notable for producing a special treasure known as a Dusk Talisman (黃昏符)[2] that assist with breaking through to the Entering Nirvana Realm. It is currently ruled by Ban Ta, its Sacred Master who has ruled for over 500,000 years.[3] Other notable features include its high number of Extraordinary Pattern Law Talents[4] and its Immortal Arts Library[5] although some of this information might not be true as it was said as part of a sales pitch. This Realm is the corpse of Purple Gold Heavenly Lord (紫金天君) Heuk Am (黑庵/Black Hermitage).

History

500,000 years ago, Sacred Master Ban Ta was captured with the help of Sacred Master Baek Woon[6] by multiple True Persons, under the orders of the Radiance Eight Immortals[7] after which he was bound to the realm until he can find a replacement. Ban Ta seems to hate the Esteemed Ones of his realm going so far as to call them "parasite['s]"[8] and disparaging them at almost every opportunity.
